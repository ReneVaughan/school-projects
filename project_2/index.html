Pine Park

Pine Park is located in Roseville, MN. We are committed to preserving local plants and wildlife. Our park houses over 93 indigenous plants and trees along with 56 native species of wildlife. Although we have many native species of wildlife our park is most popular for bird watchers!

We are currently in our winter season and most of the trails are closed for safety. Our main walking trails are still open for those who like brisk walks!


We are open Monday - Saturday!
